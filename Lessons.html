Playing Lesson

The 9 hole playing lesson is designed to help players of all levels. The focus is to help students fully understand the strategies of playing golf. Learn how to evaluate each situation and manage your game at a brand new level ! Class fee is $195 and includes cart and green fees and a one hour follow up lesson. You too can learn to play and think like a professional !

Topics of discussion include:

  • 1. Course Management
  • 2. Proper Shot and Club Selection
  • 3. Use of Pre Shot Routine
  • 4. Individual Hole Evaluation
  • 5. Basic Scoring Fundamentals
